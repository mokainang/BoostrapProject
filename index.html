About Us

If you're seeking a unique dining experience, Lizz Restaurant offers a delightful exploration of Mediterranean flavors in a luxurious setting. At Lizz, we take pride in serving a diverse array of dishes from across the Mediterranean region. Our menu is filled with both traditional and modern recipes that are sure to satisfy even the most discerning palates. We use only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking techniques to ensure every meal is bursting with flavor. Indulge in our innovative fine dining cuisine in a relaxed atmosphere, or enjoy a sumptuous afternoon tea in one of our bright, airy lounges. Spend leisurely hours with loved ones over a delightful Sunday lunch, or grab a quick bite from our bar menu. Whatever your preferences, our menu has something to delight you. Step into the world of Mediterranean cuisine at Lizz Restaurant.
